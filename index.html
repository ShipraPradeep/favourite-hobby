My Favourite Hobby
READING BOOKS

Hi!

My name is Shipra Pradeep. I am student of class 6th. Reading is my favourite hobby for many reasons. It provides us with an abundance of knowledge and information. The second reason for my liking of books is that it provides so much scope for the imagination. We can let our mind wander into the depths of the book and interact with its' characters. It also significantly improves our vocabulary. Reading helps us to develop and work on ourselves and makes us a succesful along with providing entertainment and immense pleasure.
